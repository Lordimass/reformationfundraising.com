24HR STREAM 2021

The 2021 livestream was a 24 hour stream to raise money for Mental Health UK. This stream was done as part of a school event where teams of people had to raise as much money as possible to win points for their house, our team won by a couple of orders of magnitude. Due to the fact that it was a school managed event, the donations page was not managed through Tiltify like in 2020, we have since managed to locate the donation logs, but for that reason won't be sharing the page out of respect for everyone's privacy, but the VOD is published below!
